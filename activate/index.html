How do i activate my5 on tv ?

To activate my5 on tv, the process is quite simple. The official website to activate my5 and start watching on-demand content is my5.tv/activate .

1st Step Install My5 App on Your TV.

  1. Get your device, whether it is a Smart TV, Roku, Amazon Fire Stick, or any other streaming device.
  2. Go to the App Store.
  3. Search for My5 and download the app for free.

2nd Step Open My5 and Get Activation Code.

  1. Launch the My5 application on your television set top box or other compatible device.
  2. Select the Sign In or Activate button.
  3. An activation code will appear on the screen.

3rd Step Visit my5.tv/activate .

  1. From your computer or any other mobile browser, go to My5.tv/activate and enter the activation code.
  2. Sign in to your My5 account or sign up if you don’t have one yet.

4th Step Enter the Activation Code.

  1. Type the code that you got from your television and enter it in the website.
  2. Then select the Activate button and wait for the process to finish.

5th Step Begin to Watch My5 on TV.

  1. When the activation is complete, your TV screen will update itself automatically.
  2. You will be able to discover and watch My5’s content without a hitch.
