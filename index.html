How do i reset my5 password ?

my5.tv/activate is for activating My5 on your device. or if you need to reset your My5 password keep useing this portal, follow steps below. Resetting your password ensures uninterrupted access to your favorite shows on My5. For further assistance, visit My5 support.

Mobile & Tablet

1. In the top right cornner, press the user icon

2. Below the password input press the link 'Forgotten your password?'

3. Enter your email address in input field and press 'Submit' . How to reset your password with instructions An email will be sent to you.

4. In the email press the 'Reset link' .

5. Choose new password and confirm a new password.

Computer

2. Below the password input press the link 'Forgotten your password?'

3. Enter your email address in input field and press 'Submit' . How to reset your password with instructions An email will be sent to you.
