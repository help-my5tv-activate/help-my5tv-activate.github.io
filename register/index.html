How do i register on my5 ?

To register my5 on tv, the process is quite simple. Create account on my5 and start watching on-demand content by activate my5 with my5.tv/activate .

Android Mobile & Tablet

1. In the bottom right cornner, press the more icon

2. Now you are On the settings page, press "Sign In" find on top right cornner

3. Enter your details to complete to the Register an account.

4. Read the terms and conditions carefully.

5. Press 'Register'.

Computer

2. Enter your details to complete to the Register an account.

3. Read the terms and conditions carefully.

4. Press "Create account".
